For you, on your birthday

I wrote this slowly. Read it slowly too.

Happy sweet sixteen, pretty. I don’t know how to make this sound simple, so I won’t try.

Sixteen sounds soft, but you aren’t fragile. You’ve lived through things most people never talk about, and somehow you’re still kind, still gentle, still you.

This year was heavy. I know there were days you woke up tired already, nights where you had to be your own comfort. And yet, you kept going. Quietly. Bravely.

I’m so proud of you. Not just for surviving, but for staying warm in a world that tried to harden you.

You’re going to be an incredible psychologist someday. You listen with your whole heart. You make people feel seen. That’s not something you learn. That’s who you are.

Please remember this, okay? You don’t have to be strong all the time. You don’t have to carry everything alone.

I’m here. On the easy days, and the quiet hard ones too. No pressure. No conditions. Just here.

You are loved. Deeply. Softly. Truly.
